About Me

I'm Aashish Chand, a passionate photographer with over 5 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey in photography began with a simple love for visual storytelling and has evolved into a professional career dedicated to creating timeless images that tell unique stories.

Specializing in portrait photography, landscape shots, and creative compositions, I believe every photograph should evoke emotion and tell a story. My work has been featured in various exhibitions and publications, and I've had the privilege of working with clients from all walks of life.
